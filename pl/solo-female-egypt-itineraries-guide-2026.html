Protokół rezerwacji zakwaterowania

  • Book direct with hotel: Ensures special requests (female floor, secure room) are properly noted and confirmed
  • Confirm safety features in writing: Email confirmation of female-only floor assignment, security protocols, and female staff availability
  • Verify location: Use Google Maps to confirm neighborhood safety and proximity to attractions/transport
  • Read recent reviews: Filter for "solo female traveler" reviews on Booking.com/Hostelworld for authentic feedback
  • Consider Egypt Photography Tours booking: We pre-coordinate all safety features and provide backup accommodation options if needed

Zakwaterowanie Bezpieczeństwo Czerwone flagi

Avoid: Hotels in isolated areas, properties without 24/7 reception, accommodations with poor recent reviews from solo female travelers.
Verify: Security measures, female staff availability, neighborhood safety, and transport access before finalizing booking.
