Hoofdstuk 2: Terrorisme Bedreigingen in Egypte - Realiteit versus Perceptie

Terrorismerisicobeoordeling 2025/2026

De terroristische dreiging in Egypte is aanzienlijk afgenomen sinds 2018, met 2025 tonen de laagste incidenten in een decennium. Voor geïnformeerde reisbeslissingen is het echter essentieel om het werkelijke risicoprofiel te begrijpen.

Belangrijke context: Geografische concentratie

Over 95% of terrorism incidents occur in North Sinai, approximately 400km from the nearest tourist destination. This remote desert region near the Gaza border has no tourist attractions and is completely avoided by reputable tour operators.

Terrorismepreventiemaatregelen op toeristische plaatsen

Egypte heeft op alle belangrijke toeristische bestemmingen een meerlagige veiligheidsaanpak toegepast:

  • Tourist Police Deployment: Over 15,000 specially trained officers stationed at archaeological sites, hotels, and tourist areas
  • Vehicle Checkpoints: All tourist transport undergoes security screening before entering site perimeters
  • Surveillance Systems: Advanced CCTV with facial recognition at major sites like Giza Pyramids and Karnak Temple
  • Armed Escorts: Convoys with armed tourist police for desert excursions and remote temples
  • Airport Security: Enhanced screening exceeding international standards at all tourist airports
  • Intelligence Monitoring: Real-time threat assessment coordination between tourism police and intelligence agencies

Prevention: Book with reputable companies like Egypt Photography Tours where prices are fixed and transparent. For independent purchases, confirm prices in writing, use official taxis with meters, and be prepared to politely walk away from aggressive vendors.

Prevention: Real tourist police wear distinctive uniforms with identification numbers. They never ask for money. Egypt Photography Tours guides verify all official interactions.

Hoofdstuk 7: Culturele veiligheid en sociale overwegingen

Egyptische cultuur veilig en respectvol navigeren

Cultureel begrip vergroot zowel veiligheid als ervaring. Egyptische samenleving is over het algemeen conservatief en waardeert gastvrijheid, maar misverstanden kunnen ongemakkelijke situaties veroorzaken.

Richtsnoeren kledingcode

For Women: Shoulders and knees covered in public areas, looser clothing preferred. Swimwear only at resort pools/beaches.

For Men: Shorts acceptable in tourist areas, but long pants preferred for religious sites. Remove shoes when entering mosques.

Religious Sites: Women should carry a scarf for mosque visits. Both genders need modest covering.

Sociale interacties & Etiquette

  • Greetings: Handshakes common, but wait for women to initiate
  • Photographing People: Always ask permission first, especially for women
  • Religious Sensitivity: Avoid criticism of Islam or political discussions
  • Hospitality: Accepting tea or coffee is polite but not mandatory
  • Bargaining: Expected in markets but maintain polite demeanor
"The single most important safety tip for cultural interactions is simple: when in doubt, smile politely and defer to your guide. Egyptians are famously hospitable but cultural norms differ significantly from Western countries."
- Hossam, Stichter & Chief Egyptoloog
