Private Pyramids Tour Experience

Immerse yourself in the wonders of ancient Egypt with our exclusive private Pyramids tour. This carefully crafted experience combines luxury travel with deep historical exploration and professional photography. Our expert guides, who are both licensed Egyptologists and professional photographers, will take you on a journey through time while ensuring you capture stunning images of these iconic monuments.

This isn't just a tour - it's a comprehensive Egyptian experience that blends ancient history, local culture, and professional photography. We'll take you beyond the typical tourist routes to discover hidden vantage points and share insights about Egyptian civilization that you won't find in guidebooks.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore the Great Pyramid of Giza, Pyramid of Khafre, and Pyramid of Menkaure
  • Get up close with the enigmatic Sphinx and visit the Valley Temple
  • Discover hidden photo spots at the desert panorama viewpoint
  • Enjoy a dedicated professional photography session throughout the tour
  • Learn fascinating historical facts and local insights from your expert guide
  • Comfortable, air-conditioned private transportation
  • Exclusive access to less-crowded viewing areas

Inclusions

  • Professional photography service (digital photos provided post-tour)
  • Bottled water during the tour
  • Hotel pickup and drop-off in Cairo/Giza
  • All taxes and service charges
