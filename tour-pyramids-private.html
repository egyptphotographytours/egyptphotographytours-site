Private Pyramids & Sphinx Photography Tour

$250.00 / person

Embark on an exclusive journey to capture the timeless grandeur of the Giza Pyramids and the enigmatic Sphinx. This private tour is meticulously designed for photographers, ensuring optimal lighting, unique perspectives, and ample time to compose your perfect shots. Your expert Egyptologist guide will unveil the history, while our dedicated photography specialist helps you master your camera settings and find the best angles.

Discover the intricate details of ancient craftsmanship.

Tour Highlights:

  • Private air-conditioned transportation.
  • Licensed Egyptologist tour guide.
  • Dedicated photography guide/assistant.
  • Entry fees to the Giza Pyramids complex.
  • Visit to the Sphinx and its temple.
  • Optional camel ride for unique photo opportunities (additional cost).
  • Sunrise or sunset photography sessions for golden hour magic.

Capture the iconic Sphinx with expert guidance.

Inclusions:

  • All transfers by private A/C vehicle.
  • Expert English-speaking Egyptologist guide.
  • Professional photography specialist.
  • Entrance fees to specified sites.
  • Bottled water throughout the tour.

Exclusions:

  • International flights.
  • Visa to Egypt.
  • Accommodation.
  • Meals (can be arranged upon request).
  • Personal expenses and tips.
  • Entrance inside any of the Pyramids or Solar Boat Museum.

What to Bring:

  • Your camera gear (lenses, tripod, extra batteries, memory cards).
  • Comfortable walking shoes.
  • Sunscreen, hat, and sunglasses.
  • Lightweight clothing suitable for the weather.
  • Any specific photography accessories you may need.
