Your Complete Guide to Egypt Tours in 2026

Private Tours vs Group Tours in Egypt – Which Is Right for You?

Private Egypt tours offer a dedicated guide, a fully personalised itinerary and complete flexibility – you stop where you want, for as long as you want. Ideal for couples, families, photographers and anyone who values an exclusive experience. Group Egypt tours are cost-effective and social – you share the experience with a small group of like-minded travelers, led by an expert Egyptologist. Starting from $79 per person, they're perfect for solo travelers and budget-conscious visitors.

Giza Pyramids Photoshoots – Egypt's Most Iconic Photography Location

No trip to Egypt is complete without a professional photoshoot at the Great Pyramid of Khufu. Our photographers know every perfect angle, every shadow, every moment the light transforms ancient stone into cinema. Portraits, couples, families, solo shots, flat-lay travel content – all captured with professional camera equipment. Available daily from 9AM to 1PM for optimal natural light.

Luxor – Egypt's Open Air Museum

Luxor contains more ancient monuments per square kilometre than anywhere on earth. Our Luxor tours include the Valley of the Kings (Tutankhamun's tomb), Karnak Temple Complex, Hatshepsut's Mortuary Temple, the Colossi of Memnon and the illuminated Luxor Temple at night. Professional photography is included at every stop.

Aswan & Abu Simbel – The Jewel of Upper Egypt

Aswan offers Egypt's most breathtaking Nile scenery. Our tours include the Philae Temple, the Unfinished Obelisk, a Nubian village visit, the Aswan High Dam and felucca sailing at sunset. The legendary Abu Simbel Temples of Ramses II – one of humanity's greatest architectural achievements – are an unmissable add-on.

Standard Historical Sightseeing – What You Will See

  • Cairo: Grand Egyptian Museum, Egyptian Museum of Antiquities, Saladin Citadel, Khan El Khalili Bazaar, Al-Azhar Mosque, Coptic Cairo
  • Giza: Great Pyramid of Khufu, Pyramid of Khafre, Pyramid of Menkaure, Great Sphinx, Valley Temple, Solar Boat Museum
  • Luxor East Bank: Karnak Temple, Luxor Temple (day and night)
  • Luxor West Bank: Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, Colossi of Memnon, Valley of the Queens
  • Aswan: Philae Temple, Unfinished Obelisk, Aswan High Dam, Nubian village, felucca on the Nile
  • Abu Simbel: Temple of Ramses II, Temple of Nefertari – a 3,200-year-old marvel relocated to save it from the Nile
  • Alexandria: Bibliotheca Alexandrina, Qaitbay Citadel, Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa, Pompey's Pillar, Mediterranean Corniche

Best Egypt Itineraries by Trip Length

  • 3 days in Egypt: Cairo day tour, Grand Egyptian Museum, Giza Pyramids photoshoot, Sphinx and Khan El Khalili
  • 5 days in Egypt: Above + Alexandria day trip + Old Cairo Coptic quarter and Islamic mosques
  • 7 days in Egypt: Above + Luxor (Valley of Kings, Karnak) + Aswan (Abu Simbel, Philae) – the classic Egypt week
  • 10+ days: Complete Egypt with Nile cruise from Luxor to Aswan, White Desert, Siwa Oasis or Red Sea extension
