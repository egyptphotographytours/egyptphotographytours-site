Best Photo Spots in Egypt: Where We Take You

Our Egypt photography tours cover all the iconic locations plus hidden gems most tourists miss:

1. Giza Pyramids Complex

The Great Pyramid of Giza, Sphinx, and surrounding desert provide endless photographic opportunities. We know the best angles, times of day, and vantage points for stunning pyramid photos.

2. Cairo's Historic Districts

Islamic Cairo, Coptic Cairo, and modern downtown areas offer a mix of ancient architecture, vibrant street life, and cultural diversity perfect for photography.

3. Alexandria Mediterranean Coast

From the Citadel of Qaitbay to the modern Bibliotheca Alexandrina, Alexandria offers coastal beauty and Greco-Roman history.

4. Nile River & Felucca Boats

The lifeblood of Egypt provides beautiful reflections, sunset shots, and traditional boat photography opportunities.

5. Local Markets & Bazaars

Khan el-Khalili and local markets offer colorful, vibrant scenes perfect for street and people photography.

When to Visit Egypt for Photography

The best time for photography in Egypt depends on what you want to capture:

  • October-April: Pleasant temperatures, ideal for daytime photography
  • May-September: Hotter but great for early morning and late afternoon shoots
  • Golden Hour: Always magical in Egypt - we schedule tours around these times
  • Festival Seasons: Ramadan and other festivals offer unique cultural photography

Photography Tips for Egypt
