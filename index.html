Your Complete Guide to Egypt Tours in 2026

Private Tours vs Group Tours in Egypt – Which Should You Choose?

Private Egypt tours give you a dedicated guide, flexible timings, and a fully personalized itinerary – ideal for couples, families, and photographers who want to linger at their favorite sites without rushing. Group Egypt tours offer a social, budget-friendly way to explore with like-minded travelers and cost significantly less per person – great for solo backpackers and budget-conscious visitors.

At Egypt Photography Tours, we offer both options across all destinations – Cairo, Giza Pyramids, Luxor, Aswan, Alexandria and Nile cruises. Mix and match: start with a private Pyramids photoshoot, then join our group Luxor temple tour.

The Giza Pyramids – Egypt's Most Iconic Photography Destination

No trip to Egypt is complete without standing before the Great Pyramid of Khufu – the last surviving Wonder of the Ancient World. Our Pyramids photoshoot experience is our most requested service. Your personal professional photographer scouts the optimal positions, uses natural light angles and composition techniques to capture images worthy of magazine covers – of you, your partner, your family or your travel crew.

Sessions include the Pyramid of Khafre, the Great Sphinx, Pyramid of Menkaure and the panoramic desert view. Available daily from 9AM to 1PM for the best natural light.

Luxor – Egypt's Open Air Museum

Luxor is home to more ancient monuments than any city on earth. Our Luxor private and group tours cover the Valley of the Kings (including Tutankhamun's tomb), Karnak Temple Complex, Hatshepsut's Mortuary Temple, the Colossi of Memnon and the Luxor Temple illuminated at night. Photography is permitted at most sites and your professional guide captures every special moment.

Aswan & Abu Simbel – The Jewel of Upper Egypt

Aswan offers some of Egypt's most breathtaking scenery – the Philae Temple rising from the Nile, the Aswan High Dam, colorful Nubian villages and felucca sailboats gliding at sunset. Our day trips and overnight packages from Cairo include the legendary Abu Simbel Temples – one of the world's greatest architectural achievements, relocated to save them from the rising Nile.

Standard Historical Tours – Expert-Led Egypt Sightseeing

Not everyone wants a photography package – and that's perfectly fine. Our standard historical Egypt tours deliver exceptional value: expert Egyptologist commentary, hassle-free logistics, comfortable transportation and memorable visits to all the must-see sites. The Grand Egyptian Museum in Giza – home to the complete Tutankhamun collection – is a highlight of every Cairo tour.

  • Cairo Day Tour: Egyptian Museum, Citadel, Khan El Khalili, Coptic Cairo
  • Giza Full Day: Three Pyramids, Sphinx, Solar Boat Museum, Valley Temple
  • Luxor East Bank: Karnak Temple, Luxor Temple (day & night)
  • Luxor West Bank: Valley of Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, Colossi of Memnon
  • Aswan Full Day: Philae Temple, Unfinished Obelisk, High Dam, Nubian village
  • Abu Simbel: Temples of Ramses II and Nefertari – an unmissable Egyptian experience

Planning Your Egypt Trip in 2026 – Everything You Need to Know

How to Get the Most from Your Egypt Tour

Egypt's major sites are spread across three main areas – Cairo/Giza in the north, Luxor/Aswan in the south (Upper Egypt), and Alexandria on the Mediterranean coast. Most travelers fly into Cairo, explore Cairo and the Pyramids for 2–3 days, then fly or take the overnight train to Luxor and Aswan.

Best Egypt Itineraries by Trip Length

  • 3 days in Egypt: Cairo, Grand Egyptian Museum, Giza Pyramids and Sphinx, Egyptian Museum, Khan El Khalili Bazaar
  • 5 days in Egypt: Above + Alexandria day trip + Old Cairo Coptic churches and Islamic quarter
  • 7 days in Egypt: Above + fly to Luxor (Valley of Kings, Karnak), day trip to Aswan and Abu Simbel
  • 10 days in Egypt: Complete Egypt experience with Nile cruise from Luxor to Aswan + Siwa Oasis or Red Sea add-on
