Honeymoon Planning Tips & FAQs

Budget Planning for Your Egypt Honeymoon

Egypt offers excellent value for luxury travel. A comprehensive 10-12 day honeymoon with private tours, luxury accommodations, and professional photography typically costs $4,000-$7,000 per couple. Budget-friendly options (3-star hotels, group tours) start around $2,000, while ultra-luxury experiences can exceed $10,000.

Essential Documents & Preparations

  • Passports: Must be valid for at least 6 months from entry date
  • Visas: Available on arrival for most nationalities ($25 USD)
  • Vaccinations: Recommended but not required (consult your doctor)
  • Travel Insurance: Highly recommended for peace of mind
  • Booking Timeline: Book 6-9 months in advance for peak season

What to Pack for Your Egypt Honeymoon

For Her: Lightweight dresses (modest for temples), swimwear, comfortable walking shoes, sun hat, sunscreen, elegant outfit for special dinners, scarf for mosque visits.

For Him: Lightweight trousers, collared shirts, comfortable shoes, swim trunks, sunscreen, smart-casual outfit for dinners.

For Both: Camera/phone for casual photos, power adapters (Type C/F), reusable water bottles, basic first aid kit, romantic playlist for travel moments.
