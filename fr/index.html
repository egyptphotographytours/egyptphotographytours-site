The Complete Guide to Egypt Tours – Everything You Need to Know

Why Choose a Private Egypt Tour?

Private Egypt toursoffer a dedicated certified guide, a fully personalised itinerary and complete flexibility – you stop where you want, stay as long as you like and visit sites at the ideal time of day. Ideal for couples, families, solo travelers, photographers and anyone who values an exclusive, unhurried experience without compromise. Private tours can be booked for any group size, from a solo traveler to a corporate delegation of 50.

Group Tours in Egypt – Social, Affordable & Excellent

Group Egypt toursare cost-effective, social and expertly guided. Our small-group format (maximum 8 people) ensures you never feel lost in a crowd – you receive the same Egyptologist expertise as a private tour but share the experience with like-minded travelers from around the world. Group tours are perfect for solo travelers, budget-conscious visitors and backpackers. Starting from just $79 per person with all entrance fees, certified guide and air-conditioned transport included.

Giza Pyramids Photography Tours – Egypt's Most Iconic Experience

No trip to Egypt is complete without aprofessional photoshoot at the Great Pyramid of Khufu. Our award-winning photographers know every perfect angle, golden-hour moment, and composition that transforms ancient stone into cinematic art. Portraits, couples, families, solo shots, content creator reels, flat-lay travel photography – all captured with professional full-frame camera equipment. Available daily from early morning for the best natural Sahara light. You receive 150–300 fully edited, high-resolution images delivered to your Google Drive or Dropbox within 48 hours.

Luxor – Egypt's Open-Air Museum & Greatest Temple City

Luxor is often called the world's greatest open-air museum, containing more ancient monuments per square kilometre than anywhere else on earth. OurLuxor historical toursinclude the magnificentValley of the Kings(Tutankhamun's tomb, Ramses II and more), the colossalKarnak Temple Complex– the world's largest ancient religious site –Hatshepsut's Mortuary Temple, theColossi of Memnonand the spectacularly illuminatedLuxor Temple at night. Professional photography is included at every stop.

Aswan & Abu Simbel – The Jewel of Upper Egypt

Aswan offers Egypt's most breathtaking Nile scenery – granite boulders, lush islands, Nubian villages and endless blue sky. OurAswan toursinclude the enchantingPhilae Templededicated to the goddess Isis, the extraordinaryUnfinished Obelisk, an authenticNubian village experience, theAswan High Damand magical felucca sailing at sunset. The legendaryAbu Simbel Templesof Ramses II – humanity's greatest engineering achievement and a marvel of ancient architecture – are an unmissable add-on that takes your breath away.

Cairo – A City Where 5,000 Years of History Live

Cairo is one of the world's most extraordinary cities, home to the greatest concentration of ancient monuments alongside a vibrant modern metropolis. OurCairo historical tourscover the revolutionaryGrand Egyptian Museumin Giza (the world's largest archaeological museum), the historicEgyptian Museum of Antiquitieswith its legendary mummy collection, the medieval magnificence ofSaladin's Citadel, the sensory explosion ofKhan El Khalili Bazaar, the spiritual grandeur ofIslamic Cairo's ancient mosques and the early Christian treasures ofCoptic Cairo.

Alexandria – Where Greece Meets Egypt on the Mediterranean

Alexandria is a city of layers – Greek, Roman, Arab, Ottoman and modern Egypt all woven into one coastal gem on the Mediterranean. OurAlexandria day trips from Cairocover the spectacularBibliotheca Alexandrina(modern Library of Alexandria), the historicQaitbay Citadeloverlooking the sea, the underground mystery of theCatacombs of Kom El Shoqafa, the monumentalPompey's Pillarand the legendaryMediterranean Corniche. A full-day journey that adds an entirely different dimension to your Egypt experience.

Recommended Egypt Itineraries by Trip Length

  • 1–2 days in Egypt:Giza Pyramids photoshoot, Great Sphinx, Valley Temple, Grand Egyptian Museum – Cairo's most iconic highlights
  • 3 days in Egypt:Above + Islamic Cairo tour, Khan El Khalili Bazaar, Coptic Cairo, Saladin Citadel, Egyptian Museum
  • 5 days in Egypt:Above + Alexandria full-day trip with Qaitbay Citadel, Bibliotheca Alexandrina and Mediterranean Corniche
  • 7 days in Egypt:The classic complete Egypt – above + Luxor (Valley of Kings, Karnak) + Aswan (Abu Simbel, Philae) – an unforgettable week
  • 10+ days:The ultimate Egypt – above + Nile cruise from Luxor to Aswan, White Desert safari, Siwa Oasis or Red Sea extension
