الأسئلة المتكررة

What makes custom Egypt tours different from standard group tours?
Custom Egypt tours are built entirely around your specific interests, photography goals, pace preferences, and special requirements. Unlike standard group tours with fixed schedules and 15-40 people, custom tours offer complete flexibility, private guides matched to your interests, golden hour scheduling for photography, and the ability to extend time at favorite sites or add unique experiences.
How far in advance should I book a custom Egypt tour?
We recommend booking custom Egypt tours at least 3-6 months in advance, especially during peak season (October-April). This allows time for proper itinerary design, securing special access permits, booking premium accommodations, and arranging specialized guides. Last-minute bookings are possible but may limit options.
Can I customize a tour for photography purposes?
Absolutely. Our custom photography tours are specifically designed around optimal lighting conditions, including sunrise and sunset access to sites like the Pyramids and Karnak Temple. We provide equipment support, photography permits, and guides who understand both Egypt and photography needs.
Are custom Egypt tours more expensive than group tours?
Custom tours typically cost more than group tours due to private guides, dedicated transportation, and personalized service. However, the value lies in the exclusive access, flexibility, and tailored experience. We work with various budget levels and can design custom experiences at different price points.
Can families with children book custom Egypt tours?
Yes, our family custom Egypt tours are specifically designed for travelers with children. We include child-friendly Egyptologist guides, interactive activities at archaeological sites, appropriate pacing, family-friendly accommodations, and special experiences like camel rides and treasure hunts.
