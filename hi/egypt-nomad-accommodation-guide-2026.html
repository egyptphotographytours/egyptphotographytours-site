Professional Contract Strategy Guidance

फोटोग्राफी Nomads के लिए आवश्यक अनुबंध क्लॉज

Equipment Storage: Explicit permission to store photography gear and editing equipment, with security provisions for valuable items

Internet Guarantee: Minimum speed requirements (50+ Mbps download, 10+ Mbps upload) with compensation for outages affecting remote work

Flexible Terms: Month-to-month or 3-month minimum options for nomad flexibility, with reasonable notice periods for termination

Maintenance Response: Clear timelines for repairs affecting work (internet, power, AC) with escalation procedures for urgent issues

Early Termination: Reasonable exit clauses for changing travel plans, with defined penalties that don't penalize photography workflow needs

Subletting Rights: Permission to sublet during travel periods to offset costs, with landlord approval process
