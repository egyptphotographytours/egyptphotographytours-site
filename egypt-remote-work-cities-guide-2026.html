Chapter 4: Internet Speed Testing & Backup Strategies - Book with Connectivity Optimization Guidance

Reliable connectivity is non-negotiable for remote work, but Egypt's infrastructure varies significantly by location and provider. This chapter provides expert speed testing protocols and backup strategies specifically for photography-focused remote workers.

Professional Connectivity Optimization Strategies

📊

Internet Speed Testing Protocol

Pre-Booking Verification: Request speed test results from accommodations; use Ookla Speedtest or Fast.com for independent verification
Peak Hour Testing: Test speeds during your typical work hours (9am-5pm local time) to assess real-world performance
Upload Speed Focus: Prioritize upload speeds (10+ Mbps recommended) for sending large photography files to clients
Latency Consideration: Test ping times (<100ms ideal) for video calls and real-time collaboration
Book This Experience: The Cairo & Giza 2-Day Photo Tour includes connectivity verification protocols that ensure work-ready infrastructure

🔄

Reliable Backup Strategies

Mobile Hotspots: Vodafone, Orange, and Etisalat offer 4G/5G with 20-80 Mbps speeds as primary backup; purchase local SIMs upon arrival
Dual ISP Setups: Many accommodations offer fiber + DSL redundancy; request this configuration for critical work
Coworking Redundancy: Maintain memberships at multiple coworking spaces with different providers for location-based redundancy
Satellite Options: Starlink increasingly available for remote photography locations; verify coverage before travel

Time Blocking: Schedule intensive remote work during off-peak photography hours (midday) and shooting during golden hours
Location Synergy: Choose accommodations and coworking spaces that minimize travel time between work and photography locations
Equipment Logistics: Plan gear storage and transport between workspaces and shooting locations to maximize efficiency
Community Leverage: Connect with local photographers who understand both remote work demands and optimal shooting conditions
