About Egypt Photography Tours

Meet our team of expert local guides, professional photographers, and Egyptologists dedicated to helping you capture the magic of Egypt through your lens.

Our Passion: Unveiling Egypt Through Your Lens

The Egypt Photography Tours adventure began from a deep love for Egypt's timeless beauty and a strong passion for the art of photography. We believe that Egypt is more than just a destination; it's a living canvas of history, culture, and breathtaking landscapes waiting to be captured from a unique perspective. Our mission is to provide unparalleled access and guidance for photographers of all levels to capture the true essence of this beautiful country.

We are a dedicated team of local experts, including professional photographers and licensed Egyptologists, who are passionate about providing an authentic, immersive, and unforgettable experience. We don't just show you the famous spots; we unveil their stories, the hidden perspectives, and the perfect moments to capture their magic, whether it's the mysterious glow of the pyramids at sunrise or the vibrant chaos of a bustling souk.

Founded in 2015 by Hossam, a professional photographer with over 15 years of experience capturing Egypt's wonders, our company has grown to become the premier choice for photography enthusiasts seeking to document their Egyptian journey. We've helped thousands of travelers from around the world capture memories that last a lifetime.

Our Vision and Values

Authenticity

We are committed to providing an authentic Egyptian experience, connecting you with the local culture, people, and hidden gems that lie off the typical tourist path.

Expert Guidance

Our team is made up of highly skilled Egyptologists, experienced tour guides, and professional photographers who offer personalized coaching and insights.

Safety & Comfort

Your well-being is our top priority. We ensure seamless logistics, private transportation, and adherence to the highest safety standards throughout your journey.

Exclusive Access

We strive to provide unique access and timing to iconic locations, allowing you to shoot without crowds or distractions during optimal lighting conditions.

Sustainable Tourism

We believe in responsible tourism, respecting local communities and preserving Egypt's heritage for future generations through ethical practices.

Unforgettable Memories

Ultimately, our goal is not just to help you take beautiful photos, but to help you create cherished memories that will last a lifetime.

Meet Our Team

Hossam

Founder & Lead Photographer

With over 15 years of professional photography experience and a deep knowledge of Egypt's history, Hossam founded Egypt Photography Tours to share his passion for capturing Egypt's beauty. His expertise in landscape and architectural photography ensures you get the perfect shot every time.

Mohamed

Senior Egyptologist Guide

A licensed Egyptologist with a Master's degree in Egyptology, Mohamed brings ancient history to life with captivating stories and insights. His extensive knowledge of archaeological sites ensures you understand the significance of every location you photograph.

Sarah

Photography Instructor

Specializing in portrait and street photography, Sarah helps travelers capture the human element of Egypt. Her patient teaching style and technical expertise make her perfect for photographers looking to improve their skills while exploring Egypt.

Ahmed

Logistics Coordinator

Ahmed ensures every aspect of your tour runs smoothly. From transportation to accommodations and special access permissions, his attention to detail and local connections guarantee a seamless and stress-free photography experience.

Layla

Cultural Specialist

Layla's deep understanding of Egyptian culture and traditions helps travelers capture authentic moments and navigate local customs with respect. Her language skills and cultural insights enrich every photographic journey.

Karim

Adventure Photography Guide

Specializing in desert and adventure photography, Karim leads expeditions to remote locations like the White Desert and Sinai. His survival skills and photography expertise make him the perfect guide for off-the-beaten-path photographic adventures.

Why Choose Egypt Photography Tours?

When you choose Egypt Photography Tours, you're not just booking a vacation - you're investing in a transformative photographic journey guided by experts who are as passionate about Egypt as they are about photography.

Our unique approach combines:

  • Local Expertise: Our guides are Egyptian-born with deep cultural and historical knowledge
  • Photographic Excellence: Professional photographers who understand lighting, composition, and timing
  • Exclusive Access: Relationships with local sites for optimal shooting conditions
  • Personalized Attention: Small group sizes and private tours for tailored experiences
  • Comprehensive Support: From equipment advice to post-processing tips

We've hosted photographers from beginners with smartphones to professionals with extensive gear, and we tailor each experience to match your skill level and photographic goals.
