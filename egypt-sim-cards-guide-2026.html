Reliable connectivity is non-negotiable for remote work, but Egypt's infrastructure can be unpredictable. This chapter provides expert backup strategies specifically for photography-focused remote workers.

Professional Backup Strategy Guidance

🔄

Essential Backup Connectivity Options

Dual SIM Setup: Primary Vodafone + secondary Orange/Etisalat for redundancy in different areas • Cost: $15-75/month combined • Best for: Urban areas with provider-specific coverage variations
Mobile Hotspot Devices: Dedicated 4G/5G hotspots with higher data allowances for critical work sessions • Cost: $20-50/month + device • Best for: Heavy upload sessions, client presentations
Satellite Options: Starlink increasingly available for desert/remote photography locations • Cost: $100-150/month + equipment • Best for: Critical work in areas with no cellular coverage
Coworking Redundancy: Maintain memberships at multiple coworking spaces with different ISPs for location-based backup • Cost: $50-150/month combined • Best for: Urban remote work with flexible location options
