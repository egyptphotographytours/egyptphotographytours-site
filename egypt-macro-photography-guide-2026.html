Chapter 4: Professional Equipment Guidance - Book Tours with Gear Support

Choosing the right equipment balances optical performance with durability for Egypt's dust, heat, and bright light. When you book with Egypt Photography Tours, we provide expert equipment recommendations and protection strategies.

Professional Macro Equipment Support

Recommended Macro Lenses (Provided or Recommended on Our Tours)

90-105mm f/2.8 Macro (Top Choice): Ideal working distance for hieroglyphs and stone textures; weather-sealed versions resist dust. Available on all professional tours

60mm f/2.8 Macro: Wider angle captures context with market spices and crafts; lighter weight for all-day carrying. Perfect for Cairo & Giza tours

150-180mm Macro: Extended reach for desert flora and distant details; requires sturdy tripod. Featured on 7-Day Adventure tours

All tours include professional equipment guidance and protection strategies for Egypt's challenging conditions.

Essential Accessories Included or Recommended on Our Tours

  • Circular Polarizer: Reduces glare on stone surfaces and spice piles; enhances sky contrast in desert scenes
  • Focus Rail: Enables precise focus stacking for deep hieroglyph carvings and complex textures
  • Portable Reflector: 5-in-1 collapsible reflector fills shadows in markets and desert without adding flash complexity
  • Mini Tripod: Tabletop tripod stabilizes macro shots on uneven temple floors or desert surfaces
  • Lens Cleaning Kit: Air blower, microfiber cloths, lens pen, and sensor cleaning tools for daily dust management

Professional Camera Settings Guidance on Our Tours

  • Aperture: f/8-f/11 for optimal sharpness and depth; stop down to f/16 for focus stacking
  • Shutter Speed: 1/125s minimum handheld; use tripod for slower speeds in low-light markets
  • ISO: Base ISO 100 for stone textures; increase to 800-3200 for market interiors as needed
  • Focus Mode: Manual focus with focus peaking for precise control on textures; use focus magnification
  • File Format: Always shoot RAW for maximum texture detail recovery and white balance flexibility
