Why Alexandria Stands Out in Egyptian Tourism

Alexandria, Egypt's "Pearl of the Mediterranean," offers a completely different experience from Cairo and Giza. Founded by Alexander the Great in 331 BC, this coastal city blends Greco-Roman history with modern Egyptian culture in ways that captivate every visitor. Our Alexandria photography tour isn't just about visiting sites - it's about capturing the soul of this unique city where ancient history meets Mediterranean charm.

Unique Benefits of Our Alexandria Tour:

  • Professional Photography Included: Unlike standard tours, we provide expert photography services at optimal locations and times
  • Local Expert Guides: Our guides are Alexandria natives who know hidden gems and local stories
  • Flexible Scheduling: Private tours allow adjustments based on weather, crowds, and your interests
  • Cultural Immersion: Experience authentic Alexandria beyond tourist areas
  • Comfort & Convenience: Private transportation and pre-arranged logistics for stress-free exploration

Comprehensive Alexandria Tour Experience

Our full-day Alexandria tour covers the city's most significant sites while providing ample time for photography and cultural experiences:

Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa: Explore this remarkable Roman necropolis where Egyptian, Greek, and Roman architectural styles merge. Our photography experts know the best angles to capture these underground wonders without the crowds that plague larger tour groups.

Pompey's Pillar & Serapeum: Stand before this 27-meter red granite column, one of the largest ancient monoliths. We time our visit for optimal lighting conditions for photography and provide historical context that brings the site to life.

Bibliotheca Alexandrina: Experience the modern revival of the ancient Library of Alexandria. Our guides explain the architectural symbolism while our photographers capture you against this stunning modern masterpiece.

Citadel of Qaitbay: Built on the site of the ancient Lighthouse (one of the Seven Wonders), this 15th-century fortress offers breathtaking Mediterranean views. We schedule our visit for golden hour photography when the stone glows in the warm light.

Alexandria Corniche: Stroll along this famous waterfront promenade, experiencing local life and capturing coastal scenes. We know the best spots for people-watching and landscape photography.

Extending Your Egyptian Adventure: Cairo & Giza

While Alexandria offers Mediterranean charm, Cairo and Giza represent the heart of ancient Egypt. Our comprehensive Egypt photography tours can be customized to include:

Giza Pyramids Private Tour: Experience the pyramids without the crowds. Our early morning access allows for crowd-free photography at the Great Pyramid, Sphinx, and surrounding complexes. We know secret vantage points that most tourists never discover.

Cairo Cultural Immersion: Explore Islamic Cairo's historic mosques, Coptic Cairo's ancient churches, and Khan el-Khalili's vibrant markets. Our photography tours capture the essence of Cairo's living history while respecting local customs.

Egyptian Museum & NMEC: Skip-the-line access to Egypt's premier museums with expert guidance on the most significant artifacts. Our photographers know how to capture museum interiors despite challenging lighting conditions.

Practical Information for Your Egypt Tour

Best Time to Visit: October to April offers pleasant weather for Alexandria's coastal exploration. Summer visits are possible with early starts and air-conditioned comfort.

What to Pack: Comfortable walking shoes, layered clothing (Alexandria can be breezy), sun protection, camera equipment (we provide recommendations), and modest clothing for religious sites.

Health & Safety: We monitor local conditions, provide sanitized transportation, and choose reputable restaurants. Travel insurance is recommended.

Cultural Considerations: Our guides provide context on local customs, appropriate dress, and respectful photography practices.
