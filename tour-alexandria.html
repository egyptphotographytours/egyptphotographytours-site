Alexandria: Full-Day Private Photography Tour from Cairo

Embark on a fascinating journey to Alexandria, Egypt's second-largest city, known as the "Pearl of the Mediterranean." This full-day private tour from Cairo offers a unique opportunity to explore its Roman and Greek heritage, magnificent Library, and picturesque seaside, all while capturing stunning photographs with guidance from your expert tour leader.

Tour Highlights:

  • ✔️ Visit the Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa, a unique Roman burial site.
  • ✔️ Explore Pompey's Pillar, a grand Roman triumphal column.
  • ✔️ Discover the modern Bibliotheca Alexandrina, a tribute to the ancient Library.
  • ✔️ Marvel at the Citadel of Qaitbay, built on the site of the ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria.
  • ✔️ Drive along the beautiful Corniche (seaside promenade).
  • ✔️ Professional photography guidance and post-tour digital photo delivery.
  • ✔️ Comfortable, air-conditioned private transportation from your Cairo/Giza hotel.

Inclusions:

  • ✅ Private expert Egyptologist guide.
  • ✅ All entrance fees to mentioned sites.
  • ✅ Private, modern, air-conditioned vehicle with professional driver.
  • ✅ Professional photography service (digital photos provided post-tour).
  • ✅ Bottled water during the tour.

Exclusions:

  • ❌ Lunch (can be arranged locally).
  • ❌ Gratuities (recommended).
  • ❌ Personal expenses.
Duration: Full Day (approx. 10-12 hours)
Total Price: $0.00 USD
