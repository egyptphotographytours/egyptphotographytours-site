Alexandria: Private Full-Day Photography Tour from Cairo

Embark on a captivating journey to Alexandria, Egypt's second-largest city, known as "The Pearl of the Mediterranean." This private full-day tour, departing from Cairo, offers a unique opportunity to explore its Roman and Greek heritage, magnificent library, and beautiful waterfront, all while capturing stunning photos with the guidance of an expert.

Tour Highlights:

  • ✔️ Visit the Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa, a unique Roman necropolis.
  • ✔️ Explore Pompey's Pillar, a majestic Roman triumphal column.
  • ✔️ Discover the modern Bibliotheca Alexandrina, a tribute to the ancient library.
  • ✔️ Admire the Citadel of Qaitbay, built on the site of the ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria.
  • ✔️ Drive along the beautiful Corniche (waterfront).
  • ✔️ Receive professional photography guidance and digital photos of your journey after the tour.
  • ✔️ Enjoy a comfortable, private, air-conditioned vehicle with a professional driver, including pick-up and drop-off from your hotel in Cairo/Giza.

What's Included:

  • ✅ Private Egyptologist guide.
  • ✅ Entrance fees to all mentioned sites.
  • ✅ Private, modern, air-conditioned vehicle with a professional driver.
  • ✅ Professional photography service (digital photos will be provided after the tour).
  • ✅ Bottled water during the tour.

What's Excluded:

  • ❌ Lunch (can be arranged on site).
  • ❌ Tipping (recommended).
  • ❌ Personal expenses.
Duration: Full-Day (approx. 10-12 hours)
Total Price: $0.00 USD
