Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a photography tour?

Is Egypt expensive to visit?

Egypt can be very affordable compared to many countries. Costs vary depending on your travel style: Budget travel: very affordable Mid-range: great value for money Luxury: high-end experiences at lower prices than Europe or the USA

What are your business hours?

We are available Monday through Sunday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (EET). We respond to inquiries 7 days a week.

Do you offer private photography tours?

Yes, we specialize in private photography tours tailored to your interests and skill level. All tours can be customized to your preferences.

What is the best time to visit Egypt?

The best time to visit Egypt is from October to April, when the weather is cooler and more comfortable for sightseeing. Summers (May–September) can be very hot, especially in southern cities like Luxor and Aswan.
