Professional Safe Food Analysis

Safe Foods' Premier Guidelines for Travelers

Cooked Vegetables: Well-cooked vegetables like carrots, zucchini, and potatoes • Health benefit: Reduces risk of food-borne illnesses while providing nutrition
Peeled Fruits: Bananas, oranges, and other fruits you can peel yourself • Health benefit: Minimizes exposure to contaminated surfaces
Hot, Freshly Cooked Meals: Dishes served piping hot from reputable establishments • Health benefit: Heat kills most pathogens that cause stomach issues
Bottled Water & Beverages: Sealed, commercially bottled water and drinks • Health benefit: Prevents water-borne illnesses
Bread & Grains: Freshly baked bread, rice, and pasta from reputable sources • Health benefit: Generally safe when properly prepared
