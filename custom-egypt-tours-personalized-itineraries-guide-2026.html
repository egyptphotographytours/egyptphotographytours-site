Key Elements of a Perfect Personalized Itinerary

1. Photography-Centric Planning

For photography enthusiasts, timing is everything. We schedule visits to iconic sites like the Pyramids, Karnak Temple, and Abu Simbel during golden hour when the light is perfect for photography. We also consider:

  • Tripod-friendly locations with space for equipment setup
  • Low-crowd time slots for uninterrupted shooting
  • Seasonal considerations for optimal lighting conditions
  • Special access for photography permits where required
  • Guides who understand photography needs and can assist with local knowledge

2. Pace and Fitness Level Considerations

Whether you're an avid hiker wanting to explore desert wadis or prefer a more leisurely pace with ample rest breaks, we tailor the daily schedule accordingly. Our custom tours accommodate:

  • Extended time at favorite sites without rushing
  • Rest days or lighter activity days as needed
  • Accessibility considerations for all mobility levels
  • Climate-appropriate scheduling (avoiding midday heat in summer)
  • Transportation choices based on comfort preferences

Pro Tip: The 70/30 Rule for Custom Itineraries

We recommend structuring custom Egypt tours with 70% pre-planned activities and 30% flexible time. This balance ensures you experience Egypt's highlights efficiently while maintaining spontaneity for unexpected discoveries or simply relaxing at a favorite location longer than planned.

  • 6-12 Months Before: Ideal for luxury custom tours, peak season travel (Oct-Apr), or complex multi-destination itineraries

Pro Tip: Seasonal Considerations for Custom Tours

Peak season (October-April) requires earlier planning and commands premium pricing. Shoulder months (May, September) offer better value with still-pleasant weather. Summer months (June-August) provide the most flexibility and value for custom tours, though heat must be considered in itinerary planning.
