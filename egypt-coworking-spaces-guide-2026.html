Professional Amenity Analysis

Essential Photography Coworking Amenities

Equipment Storage: Secure lockers or dedicated storage for cameras, lenses, tripods, and accessories - critical for protecting valuable gear

High Upload Speeds: 10+ Mbps upload minimum for sending large RAW files to clients; 20+ Mbps preferred for efficient workflow

Flexible Hours: 24/7 access or extended hours to accommodate golden hour shooting schedules and editing sessions

Creative Community: Networking with other photographers and creatives for collaboration, location tips, and skill sharing

Backup Power: Generator or UPS systems to prevent work loss during Egypt's occasional power fluctuations

Editing Stations: Color-calibrated monitors and ergonomic setups for professional photo editing

Printing Services: On-site or partnered printing for client proofs, portfolios, or exhibition preparations
