Tullens Premier riktlinjer för resenärer

Prescription Documentation: Carry original prescription with doctor's note in English and Arabic • Legal benefit: Ensures compliance with Egyptian customs regulations
Medication Quantity: Bring only personal-use quantities (typically 30-day supply) • Legal benefit: Avoids suspicion of commercial importation
Original Packaging: Keep medications in original containers with pharmacy labels • Legal benefit: Verifies authenticity and proper prescription
Controlled Substances: Special permits required for narcotics, psychotropics, or controlled medications • Legal benefit: Prevents legal complications with restricted medications
Declaration: Declare all medications at customs upon arrival • Legal benefit: Ensures transparency and compliance with Egyptian law
