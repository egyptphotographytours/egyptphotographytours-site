Ultimate 7-Day Egypt Discovery Photography Tour

Experience the ultimate Egyptian adventure designed for photographers! This comprehensive tour takes you through Cairo, Giza, Alexandria, and Luxor. Capture stunning images of ancient wonders with expert photography guidance.

Tour Highlights

  • Professional photography in each location (edited high-resolution photos)
  • Private, air-conditioned transportation throughout the tour
  • Transfers between Cairo, Giza, Alexandria & Luxor
  • Egyptologist guide at historical sites
  • Entrance tickets to all mentioned attractions
  • Bottled water during excursions
  • Assistance with best recommendations during the trip

Detailed Itinerary: 7-Day Egypt Itinerary: Cairo, Giza, Alexandria & Luxor

Day 1: Arrival in Cairo
Welcome to Egypt!

Upon arrival at Cairo International Airport, meet your tour representative for assistance.

Private transfer to your hotel

Private transfer to your hotel in Cairo for check-in.

Evening at leisure

Evening at leisure to relax after your flight or take a short walking tour of downtown Cairo to experience the city’s vibrant atmosphere. Overnight stay in Cairo.

Day 2: Giza Pyramids, Sphinx & Egyptian Museum
Giza Plateau Exploration

After breakfast, begin your day at the Giza Plateau, home to the Great Pyramid of Khufu (Cheops), Pyramid of Khafre, and Pyramid of Menkaure – the last surviving wonder of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Great Sphinx & Valley Temple

Explore the Great Sphinx of Giza and the Valley Temple, where ancient mummification rituals were performed.

Optional Photo Opportunity

Optional camel or horseback ride with stunning panoramic views of the pyramids.

Egyptian Museum Visit

Afternoon visit to the Egyptian Museum in Tahrir Square (or the new Grand Egyptian Museum if open), housing the golden treasures of King Tutankhamun and thousands of ancient artifacts. Evening transfer back to hotel in Cairo.

Day 3: Islamic & Coptic Cairo Tour
Citadel of Salah El-Din

Morning visit to the Citadel of Salah El-Din, a medieval Islamic fortress offering breathtaking views over Cairo. Explore the impressive Mosque of Muhammad Ali (Alabaster Mosque).

Coptic Cairo Exploration

Continue to Coptic Cairo to see the Hanging Church (El Muallaqa), Abu Serga Church (built above a cave believed to have sheltered the Holy Family), and the historic Ben Ezra Synagogue.

Khan El Khalili Bazaar

Afternoon stroll through Khan El Khalili Bazaar, Cairo’s most famous souq, filled with spices, lamps, souvenirs, and local crafts. Enjoy a traditional Egyptian tea or coffee at the historic El Fishawy Café. Overnight stay in Cairo.

Day 4: Alexandria Day Trip
Travel to Alexandria

Early morning transfer by private car to Alexandria, Egypt’s second-largest city and a jewel of the Mediterranean.

Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa

Visit the Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa, one of the Seven Wonders of the Middle Ages, blending Pharaonic, Roman, and Greek styles.

Qaitbay Citadel & Bibliotheca Alexandrina

Explore the impressive Qaitbay Citadel, built on the site of the legendary Lighthouse of Alexandria (Pharos), and discover the modern Bibliotheca Alexandrina. Stroll along the Corniche and enjoy fresh seafood. Evening return to Cairo for overnight stay.

Day 5: Luxor West Bank: Valley of the Kings
Morning Transfer to Luxor

Morning transfer to Luxor, the world’s greatest open-air museum. Cross the Nile to the West Bank of Luxor.

Valley of the Kings

Explore the Valley of the Kings, where pharaohs including Tutankhamun, Ramses II, and Seti I were buried in richly decorated tombs.

Temple of Queen Hatshepsut & Colossi of Memnon

Visit the magnificent Temple of Queen Hatshepsut and stop at the Colossi of Memnon. Evening optional Sound & Light Show at Karnak Temple. Overnight stay in Luxor.

Day 6: Luxor East Bank: Karnak & Luxor Temples
Karnak Temple Complex

Visit the awe-inspiring Karnak Temple Complex, the largest religious structure ever built, dedicated to the god Amun-Ra. Walk along the Great Hypostyle Hall.

Luxor Temple

Continue to Luxor Temple, beautifully illuminated if visited around sunset, originally connected to Karnak by the Avenue of Sphinxes.

Afternoon leisure

Afternoon at leisure—optional felucca sailboat ride on the Nile River or relaxing by the hotel pool. Overnight stay in Luxor.

Day 7: Return to Cairo & Departure
Morning Transfer to Cairo

Morning transfer back to Cairo.

Free time & Last-minute activities

Free time for last-minute shopping, a quick visit to the Islamic Cairo mosques, or relaxing before departure.

Departure

Transfer to Cairo International Airport for your outbound flight.
