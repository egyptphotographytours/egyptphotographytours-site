Detailed Itinerary: 7-Day Egypt Discovery Tour

Day 1: Arrival in Cairo – Welcome to Egypt

Welcome to Egypt!

Upon arrival at Cairo International Airport, meet your tour representative for assistance with luggage and customs procedures. Enjoy a VIP fast-track service through immigration.

Private Transfer to Your Hotel

Private air-conditioned vehicle transfer to your selected hotel in Cairo. During the transfer, your guide provides an initial orientation to Cairo's geography and practical travel tips.

Hotel Check-in & Evening at Leisure

Check into your hotel and settle in. Evening at leisure to relax after your flight. Optional activities: Short walking tour of downtown Cairo or Nile dinner cruise.

Day 2: Giza Pyramids, Sphinx & Egyptian Museum

Morning: Giza Plateau Exploration

Begin your day at the Giza Plateau, home to the Great Pyramid of Khufu, Pyramid of Khafre, and Pyramid of Menkaure – the last surviving wonder of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Professional Photoshoot Session

Professional photography session at optimal locations around the pyramids. Our photographer knows the best angles, lighting conditions, and timing for stunning pyramid photos.

Afternoon: Egyptian Museum Visit

Visit the Egyptian Museum in Tahrir Square (or the new Grand Egyptian Museum). Explore the world's most extensive collection of Pharaonic antiquities, including the golden treasures of King Tutankhamun.

Day 3: Islamic & Coptic Cairo Tour

Morning: Citadel of Salah El-Din

Visit the Citadel of Salah El-Din, a medieval Islamic fortress offering breathtaking views over Cairo. Explore the impressive Mosque of Muhammad Ali (Alabaster Mosque).

Coptic Cairo Exploration

Continue to Coptic Cairo to see the Hanging Church, Abu Serga Church (believed to have sheltered the Holy Family), and the historic Ben Ezra Synagogue.

Afternoon: Khan El Khalili Bazaar

Afternoon stroll through Khan El Khalili Bazaar, Cairo's most famous souk dating back to the 14th century. Enjoy traditional Egyptian tea at the historic El Fishawy Café.

Day 4: Alexandria Day Trip – Mediterranean Egypt

Morning Travel to Alexandria

Early morning transfer by private car to Alexandria, Egypt's second-largest city and a jewel of the Mediterranean. The approximately 3-hour journey includes commentary about Egypt's northern coast.

Qaitbay Citadel & Bibliotheca Alexandrina

Explore the impressive Qaitbay Citadel, built on the exact site of the legendary Lighthouse of Alexandria. Visit the modern Bibliotheca Alexandrina, a revival of the ancient Library.

Coastal Photography Session

Photography session along the Mediterranean coast. Capture Alexandria's unique blend of ancient and modern architecture against the sea backdrop.

Day 5: Luxor West Bank – Valley of the Kings

Morning Transfer to Luxor

Morning domestic flight from Cairo to Luxor. Upon arrival, meet your local Luxor guide. Luxor is often called the world's greatest open-air museum.

Valley of the Kings

Cross the Nile to the West Bank. Explore the Valley of the Kings, where pharaohs including Tutankhamun and Ramses II were buried in richly decorated tombs.

Temple of Queen Hatshepsut & Colossi of Memnon

Visit the magnificent Temple of Queen Hatshepsut at Deir el-Bahari, and stop at the Colossi of Memnon, two massive stone statues that have stood for 3,400 years.

Day 6: Luxor East Bank – Karnak & Luxor Temples

Morning: Karnak Temple Complex

Visit the awe-inspiring Karnak Temple Complex, the largest religious structure ever built, dedicated to the god Amun-Ra. Walk along the Great Hypostyle Hall with its 134 massive columns.

Luxor Temple

Continue to Luxor Temple, beautifully illuminated if visited around sunset. This temple was originally connected to Karnak by the Avenue of Sphinxes.

Afternoon Leisure & Optional Activities

Afternoon at leisure with optional activities: Felucca sailboat ride on the Nile River at sunset, visit to Luxor Museum, or relaxing by the hotel pool.

Day 7: Return to Cairo & Departure

Morning Transfer to Cairo

Morning domestic flight from Luxor back to Cairo. Depending on your international flight schedule, we optimize timing for comfort and convenience.

Free Time & Last-minute Activities

Free time in Cairo for last-minute shopping, quick visits to additional sites, or relaxing before departure.

Post-Tour Services

Within 7 days of tour completion: Delivery of edited high-resolution photos via digital download. Follow-up email with additional Egypt travel tips.
