Ultimate 7-Day Egypt Discovery Photography Tour

Experience the ultimate Egyptian adventure designed for photographers! This comprehensive tour takes you through Cairo, Luxor, and Aswan, including a magical Nile River cruise. Capture stunning images of ancient wonders with expert photography guidance.

Tour Highlights

  • Private guided tour of the sights with Egyptologist
  • Exclusive photography sessions at each historical site
  • Transfers to/from Cairo, Giza, Alexandria, and Luxor
  • Entrance fees to all mentioned sites in the itinerary
  • Professional photography guidance throughout the tour
  • Digital delivery of edited photos after the tour

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Cairo Arrival
Meet & greet at Cairo International Airport

Morning session at Giza Plateau capturing the pyramids and Sphinx in golden light.

Egyptian Museum/NMEC

Explore ancient artifacts with special access for photography.

Khan el-Khalili Bazaar

Capture vibrant market scenes and traditional crafts.

Day 2: Cairo to Luxor
Flight to Luxor

Morning transfer to airport for flight to Luxor.

Luxor Temple

Sunset photography session at Luxor Temple.

Nile Cruise Check-in

Board your luxury Nile cruise ship for 3-night journey.

Day 3: Luxor West Bank
Valley of the Kings

Morning exploration and photography in ancient tombs.

Hatshepsut Temple

Capture this unique mortuary temple against mountain backdrop.

Karnak Temple Complex

Sunset photography at Egypt's largest ancient religious site.

Days 4-7: Nile Cruise & Aswan
Nile River Photography

Capture daily life along the Nile during cruise sailing.

Edfu & Kom Ombo Temples

Visit and photograph these unique riverside temples.

Aswan Highlights

High Dam, Philae Temple, and Unfinished Obelisk photography.

Felucca Ride at Sunset

Golden hour photography during traditional boat ride.

Return to Cairo

Flight back to Cairo for international departure.
