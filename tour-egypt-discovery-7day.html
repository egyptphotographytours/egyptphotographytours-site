7-Day Egypt Discovery Tour: Cairo, Giza, Alexandria & Luxor

Experience the ultimate wonders of ancient and modern Egypt on this immersive 7-day guided private journey. From the towering Pyramids of Giza to the bustling streets of Cairo, the Mediterranean charm of Alexandria, and the majestic temples of Luxor ‚Äî this comprehensive trip brings Egypt's soul to life. Designed for travelers who want to delve deep into Egypt's history, culture, and incredible landscapes, with ample opportunities for breathtaking photography.

Detailed Itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival in Cairo & Giza Pyramids

  • Arrival at Cairo International Airport (CAI), meet & assist.
  • Private transfer to your hotel in Giza.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Giza Pyramids and the Sphinx with a professional photo session.
  • Evening: Optional Sound & Light show at the Pyramids (extra cost) or relax at the hotel.
  • Overnight in Giza.

Day 2: Cairo City Tour ‚Äì Museum & Old Cairo

  • Morning: Explore the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization (NMEC) including the Royal Mummies Hall.
  • Lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Afternoon: Discover Old Cairo, including the Hanging Church, Ben Ezra Synagogue, and the Khan el-Khalili Bazaar.
  • Overnight in Giza.

Day 3: Fly to Luxor & East Bank Exploration

  • Morning: Private transfer to Cairo Airport for your domestic flight to Luxor.
  • Upon arrival, meet your Luxor guide and transfer to your hotel.
  • Afternoon: Explore Luxor's East Bank: Karnak Temple Complex and Luxor Temple.
  • Evening: Optional Felucca ride on the Nile at sunset (extra cost).
  • Overnight in Luxor.

Day 4: Luxor West Bank & Hot Air Balloon

  • Early Morning: Optional Hot Air Balloon ride over the Valley of the Kings (highly recommended, extra cost).
  • Morning: Explore Luxor's West Bank: Valley of the Kings, Temple of Hatshepsut, and Colossi of Memnon.
  • Lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Evening: Free time or optional activities.
  • Overnight in Luxor.

Day 5: Back to Cairo & Free Afternoon

  • Morning: Private transfer to Luxor Airport for your domestic flight back to Cairo.
  • Upon arrival, private transfer to your Cairo hotel.
  • Afternoon: Free time for relaxation, last-minute souvenir shopping, or exploring nearby areas independently.
  • Overnight in Cairo.

Day 6: Alexandria Day Trip

  • Full-day private tour to Alexandria from Cairo.
  • Visit Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa, Pompey's Pillar, Citadel of Qaitbay, and Bibliotheca Alexandrina (exterior).
  • Enjoy an authentic seafood lunch by the Mediterranean.
  • Professional photography at key coastal sites.
  • Return to Cairo in the evening.
  • Overnight in Cairo.

Day 7: Departure

  • Enjoy breakfast at your hotel.
  • Depending on your flight schedule, enjoy some free time or last-minute shopping.
  • Private transfer to Cairo International Airport (CAI) for your departure flight.
  • End of services.
