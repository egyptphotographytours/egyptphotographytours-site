Detailed 7-Day Egypt Discovery Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Cairo – Welcome to Egypt

Airport Pickup & Hotel Check-in: Upon arrival at Cairo International Airport, our representative will assist you with visa stamps and luggage. Enjoy a private, air-conditioned transfer to your 4-star hotel in Giza or Downtown Cairo. Evening at leisure to rest or enjoy a optional Nile Dinner Cruise.

Day 2: Giza Pyramids, Sphinx & Grand Egyptian Museum

8:00 AM - Giza Plateau: Meet your private Egyptologist guide and photographer. Explore the Great Pyramid of Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure. Capture stunning cinematic portraits at the panoramic viewpoint and visit the enigmatic Great Sphinx and Valley Temple.

1:00 PM - Lunch: Authentic Egyptian cuisine with pyramid views.

3:00 PM - Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM): Visit the world's largest archaeological museum. See the complete Tutankhamun collection and marvel at 5,000 years of history.

Day 3: Islamic Cairo, Coptic Cairo & Khan El Khalili

9:00 AM - Citadel of Salah El-Din: Explore this medieval Islamic fortress and the stunning Mohamed Ali (Alabaster) Mosque, offering panoramic views over Cairo.

11:30 AM - Coptic Cairo: Walk through the ancient Christian quarter, visiting the Hanging Church and the Church of St. Sergius where the Holy Family rested.

2:00 PM - Khan El Khalili Bazaar: Immerse yourself in the sights, sounds, and smells of Cairo's most famous 14th-century marketplace. Enjoy traditional mint tea at the historic El Fishawy cafe.

Day 4: Alexandria Day Trip – The Mediterranean Pearl

7:00 AM - Drive to Alexandria: Scenic 3-hour drive along the desert highway to Egypt's coastal gem.

10:00 AM - Catacombs & Citadel: Explore the Roman Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa and the 15th-century Qaitbay Citadel, built on the site of the ancient Pharos Lighthouse.

1:00 PM - Seafood Lunch: Fresh Mediterranean seafood overlooking the sea.

3:00 PM - Bibliotheca Alexandrina: Visit the stunning modern revival of the ancient Library of Alexandria.

6:00 PM - Return to Cairo: Arrive back at your hotel in the evening.

Day 5: Fly to Luxor – Valley of the Kings & Hatshepsut Temple

Morning - Domestic Flight to Luxor: Fly to Luxor, the world's greatest open-air museum.

10:00 AM - West Bank Exploration: Cross the Nile to explore the magnificent Valley of the Kings, entering up to three royal tombs including the tomb of Ramses II. Visit the stunning mortuary temple of the female pharaoh Hatshepsut at Deir el-Bahari and the colossal Colossi of Memnon.

Day 6: Luxor East Bank – Karnak Temple & Nile Cruise

9:00 AM - Karnak Temple: Walk through the massive Hypostyle Hall of the Karnak Temple Complex, the largest ancient religious site in the world.

12:00 PM - Luxor Temple: Explore the beautifully preserved Luxor Temple in the heart of the city.

Afternoon - Nile Felucca or Cruise: Enjoy a peaceful sunset sail on a traditional Felucca or board a luxury Nile Cruise ship. Dinner and overnight on the Nile or in a Luxor hotel.

Day 7: Aswan, Abu Simbel & Departure

Morning - Drive to Aswan & Abu Simbel: Early morning convoy to the breathtaking Abu Simbel Temples, carved into the mountainside by Ramses II. This UNESCO masterpiece is the ultimate highlight of any Egypt tour.

Afternoon - Return & Departure: Return to Aswan or Cairo (depending on your international flight). Our team will assist you with your departure transfer from Cairo International Airport, concluding your unforgettable 7-day Egyptian adventure.
