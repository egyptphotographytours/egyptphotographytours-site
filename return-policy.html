Return, Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Egypt Photography Tours, we are committed to providing you with an unforgettable, seamless, and high-quality travel experience. Because our primary offerings consist of travel services, guided tours, and professional photography sessions, our return and refund policies are structured differently than traditional retail product returns. This policy clearly outlines your rights and our procedures regarding cancellations, refunds, and service adjustments to ensure full transparency and compliance with Google Merchant Center guidelines.

1. Tour Cancellation & Refund Policy

We understand that travel plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our clients and our local guides, drivers, and partners who reserve resources on your behalf.

  • More than 30 days before the tour start date: You are eligible for a full refund of all amounts paid, minus a standard 5% administrative and payment processing fee.
  • 15 to 30 days before the tour start date: You are eligible for a 50% refund of the total tour cost. The remaining 50% is retained to cover non-recoverable deposits paid to hotels, transport providers, and site authorities.
  • Less than 15 days before the tour start date: Tours are considered non-refundable. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made within 14 days of the scheduled start date.
  • No-Shows: If you fail to arrive for your scheduled tour or photography session without prior notice, it will be considered a "no-show," and no refund will be provided.

2. Photography Service Adjustments

Our professional photography services are a core part of our unique offering. In the rare event of a service discrepancy:

  • Photographer Unavailability: If our assigned professional photographer fails to attend the scheduled session due to company error or unforeseen circumstances on our end, you will receive a 100% refund of the photography service portion of your booking, while the guiding and transport portions of the tour will continue as planned.
  • Weather & Force Majeure: In the event of extreme weather, natural disasters, government restrictions, or other "Acts of God" that make a photoshoot or tour impossible, we will make every effort to reschedule your session at no additional cost. If rescheduling is not feasible, a partial or full refund will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis, excluding costs already irreversibly paid to third-party vendors.

3. Physical Merchandise Returns (If Applicable)

While we primarily provide services, if you purchase any physical merchandise (e.g., printed photo books, framed prints, or branded items) through our website:

  • Return Window: You may return unused, undamaged items in their original packaging within 14 days of delivery.
  • Return Shipping Costs: The customer is responsible for all return shipping costs, unless the item received is defective, damaged, or incorrect. In cases of our error, we will provide a prepaid return shipping label.
  • Condition: Items must be in the exact same condition as when received. Custom-printed or personalized items (e.g., custom photo books) are non-returnable unless they arrive with a printing defect.

4. Refund Method & Processing Timeframe

To ensure compliance and transparency, all approved refunds will be processed as follows:

  • Method: Refunds will always be issued to the original form of payment used at the time of booking (e.g., Credit Card, PayPal, or Bank Transfer). We do not issue cash refunds for online bookings.
  • Timeframe: Once your cancellation or refund request is approved, please allow 5 to 10 business days for the funds to reflect in your account. The exact timing depends on your bank or payment provider's processing speeds.

5. How to Request a Cancellation or Refund

To initiate a cancellation, return, or refund request, please contact our customer support team immediately with your Booking Reference Number and the reason for your request. We guarantee a response within 24–48 hours.
