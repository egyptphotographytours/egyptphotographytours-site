US Central (CST/CDT): 8-hour difference • Optimal overlap: 3-6pm Egypt time = 7-10am CST • Good for mid-morning client calls with buffer for afternoon golden hour shooting

US Mountain (MST/MDT): 9-hour difference • Limited overlap: 4-7pm Egypt time = 7-10am MST • Requires strategic planning but workable for critical meetings

US West Coast (PST/PDT): 10-hour difference • Challenging overlap: 5-8pm Egypt time = 7-10am PST • Best for asynchronous communication with occasional scheduled calls

Photography Integration: Structure workday to handle US client communication during overlap windows, then transition to photography during Egypt's golden hours (4-6pm) when US clients are offline
