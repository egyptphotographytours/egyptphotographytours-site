🏨 Chapter 1: The most romantic hotels in Egypt

We've combed the country to find accommodations that offer privacy, breathtaking views, and that elusive romantic spirit. Here are our top picks for 2026.

📍 Cairo & Giza

Marriott Mena House – Historic palace with direct pyramid views. Their imperial suites feature private balconies where you can watch the sound & light show while holding hands. Our Private Pyramids tour includes a sunrise photoshoot in the hotel's gardens.

Four Seasons Nile Plaza – Overlooking the Nile, with a rooftop pool and spa offering couple's hammam. The suite balconies are made for champagne at dusk.

📍 Luxor

Al Moudira Hotel – The only palace on the west bank. Arabian nights architecture, domed ceilings, orange blossom courtyards. Perfect after a day exploring the Valley of the Kings.

Sofitel Winter Palace – Victorian elegance and lush gardens. Their historic bar was a favourite of Agatha Christie.

📍 Aswan

Sofitel Legend Old Cataract – Pink granite walls, river views, and a private jetty. The spa offers couples treatments overlooking Elephantine Island. A stay here is like stepping into a romance novel.

📍 Red Sea

The Oberoi Beach Resort, Sahl Hasheesh – Arabian architecture, private plunge pools, and a house reef. Underwater wonders right from your beach.

Four Seasons Sharm El Sheikh – Secluded bays, overwater villas, and world‑class snorkeling.

🌟 Luxury Nile cruises: floating hotels for two

For the ultimate romantic voyage, board a luxury dahabiya or Nile cruiser. MS Farah and Oberoi Zahra offer all‑suite accommodations with butler service, private sundecks, and gourmet dining. Sail between Luxor and Aswan while being pampered. Our 7‑day Egypt Discovery includes a 4‑night cruise with dedicated photography stops at Edfu and Kom Ombo—just the two of you and the temples.

🌹 Chapter 2: Unforgettable experiences designed for two

Hot air balloon over Luxor – Float silently above the Valley of the Kings as the sun ignites the desert. We arrange a private basket for two with champagne and a certificate. Our photographer captures the moment from the ground and the sky.

Private Nile dinner on a felucca – A traditional sailboat transformed into your floating restaurant. Candles, fresh local delicacies, and the sound of water. Your photographer discreetly captures every glance. Add this to any Cairo tour.

Desert stargazing & camel trek – Ride into the White Desert together, then dine under a canopy of stars. Bedouin‑style camp with plush carpets and private tent. Our honeymoon tours can include this once‑in‑a‑lifetime night.
