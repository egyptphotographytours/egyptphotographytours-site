Luxury Accommodations: Egypt's Finest 5-Star Hotels

Cairo: Urban Luxury with Pyramid Views

Cairo offers some of the world's most spectacular luxury hotels, combining international standards with authentic Egyptian hospitality and breathtaking views of ancient wonders.

Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at Nile Plaza Exclusive

The epitome of luxury in Cairo, offering stunning Nile views, world-class dining, and an exclusive spa. Located in the prestigious Garden City district.

Key Features:
Infinity Pool: Heated outdoor pool with panoramic Nile views
Dining: 8 restaurants including Italian, Lebanese, and Japanese cuisine
Spa: 13,000 sq ft spa with traditional Egyptian treatments
Service: 24-hour butler service on executive floors

The St. Regis Cairo Premium

Modern elegance meets timeless luxury in this Corniche el-Nil landmark, famous for its legendary St. Regis Butler Service and sophisticated atmosphere.

Kempinski Nile Hotel Cairo Luxury

Intimate luxury with just 137 rooms and suites, all featuring private balconies with direct Nile views and exceptional personalized service.

Nile Ritz-Carlton, Cairo Exclusive

Historic elegance in the heart of downtown Cairo, featuring recently renovated rooms, multiple dining venues, and a prime location overlooking the Nile.

Giza: Luxury with Pyramid Views

For the ultimate Egyptian experience, stay in Giza with direct views of the ancient pyramids from your room.

Marriott Mena House, Cairo Exclusive

An historic luxury hotel set in 40 acres of private gardens with unparalleled views of the Great Pyramid of Giza. This iconic property has hosted royalty and celebrities for over a century.

Unique Experiences:
Pyramid Views: Many rooms offer direct views of the Great Pyramid
Historical Significance: Hosted Winston Churchill and Franklin Roosevelt during WWII
Gardens: 40 acres of lush gardens with outdoor pool
Dining: 7 restaurants including authentic Egyptian cuisine

Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at The First Residence Premium

Located adjacent to the Giza Plateau, this property offers spectacular pyramid views, especially from higher floors, with Four Seasons' signature service.

Luxor: Ancient Thebes in Modern Luxury

Luxor, the world's greatest open-air museum, offers luxury accommodations that blend seamlessly with ancient surroundings.

Sofitel Winter Palace Luxor Exclusive

A historic Victorian palace overlooking the Nile, this legendary hotel has hosted royalty and celebrities since 1886. The perfect base for exploring Luxor's ancient wonders.

Al Moudira Hotel Luxury

A unique boutique luxury property on Luxor's West Bank, featuring hand-painted domes, arches, and a stunning pool in a peaceful garden setting.

Hilton Luxor Resort & Spa Premium

Modern luxury on the banks of the Nile with spectacular views, multiple pools, and a full-service spa perfect after a day of temple exploration.

Aswan: Nubian Elegance on the Nile

Aswan's tranquil beauty is enhanced by its selection of luxury hotels offering peaceful retreats and stunning river views.

Sofitel Legend Old Cataract Aswan Exclusive

An iconic luxury hotel where Agatha Christie wrote "Death on the Nile." This historic property offers unparalleled views of the Nile's First Cataract and Elephantine Island.

Legendary Features:
Historical Legacy: Hosted Winston Churchill, Princess Diana, and Agatha Christie
Film Location: Featured in the 1978 film "Death on the Nile"
Architecture: Victorian and Moorish architecture with recent restoration
Location: Set on a hill overlooking the Nile's First Cataract

Red Sea: Luxury Beach Resorts

Egypt's Red Sea coast offers world-class luxury resorts perfect for combining ancient exploration with beach relaxation.

Four Seasons Resort Sharm El Sheikh Exclusive

A breathtaking resort set along the Red Sea coast with private beaches, multiple pools, and exceptional diving and snorkeling opportunities.

Rixos Sharm El Sheikh Luxury

Kempinski Hotel Soma Bay Premium

Sanctuary Zein Nile Chateau

An intimate luxury experience with only 12 suites, each with private balconies and butler service, offering exclusive access to less-visited sites.

Oberoi Philae Exclusive

Consistently ranked among the world's best river cruises, the Oberoi Philae offers unparalleled luxury with spacious suites, fine dining, and exceptional service.

Oberoi Philae Features:
Accommodations: 22 luxurious cabins with floor-to-ceiling windows
Dining: Gourmet restaurant with international and Egyptian cuisine
Wellness: Spa and fitness center with massage services
Amenities: Sun deck with pool and bar

Uniworld River Tosca Premium

Uniworld's custom-built River Tosca offers all-suite accommodations with marble bathrooms, a pool, and multiple dining venues while sailing between Luxor and Aswan.
