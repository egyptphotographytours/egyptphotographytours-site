2-Day Premium Private Tour: Cairo & Giza Photography Expedition

Immerse yourself in Egypt's vibrant capital and ancient wonders over two unforgettable days. This premium private tour allows you to explore the majestic Pyramids, bustling Old Cairo, and the impressive National Museum of Egyptian Civilization, all with your private guide and photographer capturing every moment. Perfect for those seeking a comprehensive and personalized experience.

  • **Day 1: Giza Pyramids & Sphinx:** Full exploration of the Giza Plateau, including the Great Pyramids and Sphinx. Optional camel ride for iconic photos. Sunset photography session.

  • Personalized insights from your expert Egyptologist guide.
  • Comfortable, air-conditioned private transportation throughout.
  • Extensive photography package with 100+ professionally edited digital photos.
