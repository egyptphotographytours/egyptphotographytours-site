Complete Destination Coverage: Everything You Want to See

Giza Plateau & Ancient Wonders

Your photography adventure begins at the Giza Plateau. We start early to beat crowds and capture the pyramids in morning light. Your professional photographer will guide you through:

  • Great Pyramid of Khufu: Perfect angles for capturing scale and majesty
  • Pyramid of Khafre: Unique photography with capstone intact
  • Pyramid of Menkaure: Intimate shots of the smallest pyramid
  • The Great Sphinx: Multiple vantage points including panoramic views
  • Solar Boat Museum: Interior photography of ancient Egyptian boat

Islamic Cairo & The Citadel

Experience the rich Islamic heritage of Cairo at the Citadel of Salah El-Din, offering panoramic city views and stunning mosque architecture photography at the Mohamed Ali Mosque (Alabaster Mosque).

Coptic Cairo & Christian Heritage

Step into the heart of Egypt's Christian heritage in Coptic Cairo. Visit the Hanging Church (Al-Muallaqa) and the Church of St. Sergius and Bacchus, believed to be where the Holy Family rested.

National Museum of Egyptian Civilization

The stunning Royal Mummies Hall at the NMEC provides a respectfully presented, climate-controlled display of Egypt's ancient rulers, offering incredible photography opportunities of these well-preserved historical figures.

Khan el-Khalili Bazaar & Old Town

Immerse in the sounds, smells, and sights of this historic Old Town market. Your guide helps navigate and find genuine souvenirs while capturing candid cultural photography in vibrant alleyways.

2026 Tour Logistics & Practical Information

Best Time for Photography Tours

October to April offers ideal weather (15-25°C) for comfortable touring. Morning and late afternoon provide the best lighting for photography. Summer months (May-September) are hotter but offer longer days and fewer crowds - we adjust schedules for early morning and late afternoon photography sessions.

What to Wear & Bring for 2026

Clothing: Comfortable walking shoes, sun protection (hat, sunglasses), layers for temperature changes. Modest dress recommended for mosque visits (shoulders and knees covered).

Photography Equipment: While we provide professional equipment, we recommend bringing your own camera for personal shots. Extra memory cards, batteries, and a lightweight tripod can be useful.
