Ortak Petty Crimes & Prevention Strategies

1. Overcharging & Price Scams

Prevention: Book with reputable companies like Egypt Photography Tours where prices are fixed and transparent. For independent purchases, confirm prices in writing, use official taxis with meters, and be prepared to politely walk away from aggressive vendors.

2. Crowded Areas

Prevention: Use money belts or secure bags in tourist-heavy areas like Khan El Khalili market. Our guides provide secure bag storage and accompany clients in crowded locations.

3. Sahte "Tourist Polis" Scams

Prevention: Real tourist police wear distinctive uniforms with identification numbers. They never ask for money. Egypt Photography Tours guides verify all official interactions.

Bölüm 7: Kültür Güvenliği ve Sosyal Bakışlar

Mısır Kültürünü Güvenli ve Saygıdeğer

Kültürel anlayış hem güvenlik hem de deneyim geliştirir. Mısır toplumu genellikle muhafazakar ve değer misafirperverdir, ancak yanlış anlaşılmalar rahatsız durumlar yaratabilir.

Elbise Kodu Kılavuzları

For Women: Shoulders and knees covered in public areas, looser clothing preferred. Swimwear only at resort pools/beaches.

For Men: Shorts acceptable in tourist areas, but long pants preferred for religious sites. Remove shoes when entering mosques.

Religious Sites: Women should carry a scarf for mosque visits. Both genders need modest covering.

Sosyal Etkileşimler ve Etiquette

  • Greetings: Handshakes common, but wait for women to initiate
  • Photographing People: Always ask permission first, especially for women
  • Religious Sensitivity: Avoid criticism of Islam or political discussions
  • Hospitality: Accepting tea or coffee is polite but not mandatory
  • Bargaining: Expected in markets but maintain polite demeanor
"The single most important safety tip for cultural interactions is simple: when in doubt, smile politely and defer to your guide. Egyptians are famously hospitable but cultural norms differ significantly from Western countries."
Hossam, Kurucu ve Şef Mısırolog
