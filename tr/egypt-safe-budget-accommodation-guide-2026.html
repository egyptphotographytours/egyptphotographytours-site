Temel Guesthouse Güvenlik Özellikleri

Secure Entry Systems: Keycard access, 24/7 reception, or secure gate systems to control property access

In-Room Safes or Lockers: Secure storage for cameras, lenses, and valuable equipment during shoots and sleep

Well-Lit Common Areas: Adequate lighting in hallways, entrances, and shared spaces for safety and comfort

Fire Safety Compliance: Clear evacuation routes, fire extinguishers, and staff trained in emergency protocols

Verified Reviews: Consistent positive mentions of safety, cleanliness, and staff reliability across multiple review platforms
